## EMF Badge Projects

[EMF Camp](https://www.emfcamp.org) is a weekend outdoor hacker and maker festival in a similar vein to the Chaos Communication Camp and the quadrennial Dutch hacker camp.

It's a long standing tradition at these kind of events to give attendees an electronic event badge with the aim of giving people interesting hardware they've probably not been exposed to before whilst being simple enough that anyone can get started writing their own software for it, or designing their own hardware add-ons for it. Usually they are some kind of microcontroller running [Micropython](https://micropython.org/).

Over the years I've built various fun hardware and software projects for the EMF Camp badges.
